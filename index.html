Lil' ole Lake Crystal

Growing up, my mom has always lived in Lake Crystal. My dad, however, has live in Lake Crystal, Mankato and also Garden City. Accoring to the same website I provided earlier, worldpopulationreview.com, Garden City has a population of 685 folks.
